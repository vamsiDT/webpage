About Me

I am currently a Research Engineer at Telecom-Paristech and a member of Newnet@paris team working under the supervision of Prof. Dario Rossi

My research is focused on Software Defined Networking (SDN), Scheduling Algorithms, High speed packet processing particularly on VPP (Vector Packet Processing) which is part of fd.io, a linux foundation project.

Work Experience

Research Engineer - Telecom-Paristech/Newnet@paris (September 2017 - Present)

Research under the supervision of Prof. Dario Rossi in collaboration with Dr. James Roberts, as part of newnet@paris team.
• The research is mainly focused on implementation of scheduling algorithms in VPP (Vector Packet Processing).
• The algorithm aims at achieving exact fair sharing of resources (CPU,Bandwidth) in a software router by fair packet dropping.

Intern - Telecom-Paristech/Newnet@paris (January - September 2017)

Bachelor thesis under the supervision of Prof.Dario and Prof.Nitin Sharma, as an exchange student from Bits-Pilani, Goa.
• Performance evaluation of VPP framework in bare-metal and virtual machines.
• Experiements on determining the optimal batch size for packet processing in VPP.
• Implementation of fairdrop algorithm in VPP for bandwidth sharing at the output interface.

Intern - GMR Varalakshmi Foundation (May - July 2015)

Internship done as part of B.E acadamic curriculum, a collaboration between Bits-pilani,Goa and GMR.
• Web development project on empowershop website.
• empowershop is an e-commerce website created as a social responsibility initiative my GMR
• Developing the website in terms of layout and organisation of the website.
